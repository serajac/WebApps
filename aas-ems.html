Why Choose AAS?

Benefits of a Degree

Go beyond certification with a college degree that opens more doors.

Higher Earning Potential

Degree holders typically earn 15-20% more than those with certification only. Many departments offer degree-based pay incentives.

Career Advancement

Supervisory positions, instructor roles, and management tracks often require or prefer candidates with college degrees.

Transfer Opportunities

Credits transfer to four-year universities for bachelor's degrees in emergency management, healthcare administration, or nursing.

Broader Knowledge Base

General education courses in communication, psychology, and anatomy provide a stronger foundation for patient care.

G3 Tuition-Free

Virginia's G3 program covers tuition and fees for eligible students, making your degree essentially free.

Job Market Advantage

Stand out in competitive hiring processes. Many fire departments and hospitals prefer degree-holding paramedics.
