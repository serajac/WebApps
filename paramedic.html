Prerequisites

Required Before Enrollment

  • Current AEMT certification (National Registry or Virginia)
  • CPR (BLS) certification for Healthcare Providers
  • Completion of AEMT courses or equivalent
  • BIO 145 - Human Anatomy and Physiology (4 credits)
  • High school diploma or GED
  • Valid driver's license

Advanced Skills You'll Master

Advanced Cardiac Care

12-lead ECG interpretation, cardioversion, transcutaneous pacing, and cardiac pharmacology.

Advanced Airway Management

Endotracheal intubation, RSI, surgical airways, and mechanical ventilation.

Pharmacology

Comprehensive medication administration including cardiac drugs, sedatives, and analgesics.

Critical Care Transport

Manage critically ill patients including ventilator patients and complex medical conditions.

Pediatric & OB Care

Neonatal resuscitation, pediatric emergencies, and obstetrical complications.

Medical Leadership

Scene management, team leadership, and critical decision-making under pressure.
