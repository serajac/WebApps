Our Programs

Build Your EMS Career Path

Progressive certification levels designed to advance your skills and career in emergency medical services.

EMT Basic

EMS 111 & EMS 120 • 1 Semester

Start your EMS journey with essential emergency care skills. Learn patient assessment, airway management, CPR, and emergency response fundamentals.

8 Credits $1,376 $37,720/yr
Learn More

Advanced EMT LOR

EMS 123-186 • 1 Semester

Advance your skills with IV therapy, advanced airway techniques, and expanded medication administration.

11 Credits $1,891 $42,077/yr
Learn More

Paramedic LOR

EMS 221-249 • 3 Semesters

Master advanced life support including cardiac monitoring, medication administration, and complex patient management.

33 Credits $5,674 $45,266/yr
Learn More

AAS in EMS

Complete Degree • 5 Semesters

Earn your Associate degree while completing Paramedic certification. Includes general education for career advancement.

65 Credits Full Degree Pathway

Why CAMP EMS

Your Success Is Our Mission

Discover what sets our EMS programs apart from the rest.

Only LOR Program in Virginia

We're the only EMS program in Virginia with Letters of Review for both AEMT and Paramedic from CoAEMSP.

Hands-On Training

Gain real-world experience with simulation labs, clinical rotations, and field internships with local fire departments.

Affordable & G3 Eligible

Community college rates plus G3 tuition assistance. Financial aid, VA benefits, and payment plans available.

Flexible Scheduling

Day and evening class options accommodate working professionals. Complete EMT in one semester.

95% Pass Rate

Our graduates consistently exceed the national average on NREMT certification exams.

Job Placement Support

Strong partnerships with Norfolk Fire-Rescue, Hampton Fire & Rescue, and area EMS agencies.

Your Path to Enrollment

Follow these simple steps to begin your EMS education journey.

1

Apply to CAMP

Complete your free application at pdc.edu/apply.

2

Meet with Advisor

Schedule an appointment with the EMS Program Director to discuss your goals.

3

Complete Requirements

Submit health forms, background check, CPR certification, and immunization records.

4

Register & Start

Register for classes, purchase textbooks, and begin your EMS journey!
