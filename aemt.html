Skills You'll Master

IV Therapy

Peripheral IV access, fluid administration, and basic IV medication delivery.

Advanced Airway

Supraglottic airways, CPAP, nebulized medications, and advanced ventilation techniques.

Cardiac Monitoring

Basic ECG interpretation, cardiac monitoring, and recognition of life-threatening dysrhythmias.

Pharmacology

Expanded medication formulary including glucose, albuterol, epinephrine, and naloxone.

Shock Management

Advanced assessment and treatment of shock states with IV fluid resuscitation.

Trauma Care

Advanced trauma assessment, hemorrhage control, and critical patient transport.

Program Requirements

  • Current EMT certification (National or Virginia State)
  • Completion of BIO 145: Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Current BLS/CPR certification
  • Background check clearance
  • Current immunizations (including TB test)
  • Physical examination
