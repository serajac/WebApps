NCLEX-RN Preparation Timeline

Structured 16-week NCLEX-RN preparation runs concurrent with NSG 270 throughout Spring 2027. Benchmarks are enforced through three ATI Comprehensive Predictor exams, daily UWorld practice, and the HURST Live Review. Students scoring below 75% probability on Predictor #3 enter mandatory remediation before ATT release.

Faculty Assignments & Clinical Ratios

All faculty assignments meet VBON 18VAC90-27 and ACEN Standard 2 qualifications (MSN minimum, DNP/PhD preferred for lead roles, current Virginia RN licensure, 2+ years recent clinical experience in the assigned specialty). Clinical ratios honor VBON maximums.
